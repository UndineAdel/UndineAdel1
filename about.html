About Us

The first years with Svalegaarden Mest

Svalegaarden Mest is wonderful to work with. The old building may be felt and it gives us a natural humility and desire to pass on the good experiences to our guests. We have found ourselves well adapted in Hasseri's work with many different angles in gastronomy. Svalegaarden Mest includes everything from private parties, meeting organization and cooking lectures.

About our History

In 1952, the beautiful grocery farm was rebuilt on the corner of Thorsens Allé and Svalegårdsvej, and the farm was then renamed to Svalegaarden. The idyllic farm is still at the same address, and has set the framework for many events and experiences over the years. In June 2016, Svalegaarden got new tenants with new spirits and bright hopes for the future - Mette and Stefan from the former Restaurant Mest. Mette and Stefan brought new energy and fresh eyes to the beautiful building, which has so far served as a corporate business.
